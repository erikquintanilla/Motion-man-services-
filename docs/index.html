How does booking work?

1. Choose a service above and pick a weekend date & time.
2. I confirm within 24 hours via email and send you a calendar file.
3. I show up on your scheduled day. Pay cash after the job is complete.

Do I need to be home during service?

For garage, yard, and patio cleanup, yes — I'll need access and may have questions about what to keep or toss. For event setup, coordination is helpful but not required once we discuss the plan.

What if it rains on my scheduled day?

I'll contact you 24 hours before if heavy rain is forecasted. We'll reschedule at no charge to another weekend that works for you. No cancellation fees.

Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?

Yes! All cleaning supplies, tools, bags, and equipment are included in the price. You don't need to provide anything.

How do I pay?

Cash payment after the job is complete. No upfront payment required. I'll confirm the exact price before starting work.

Can I book for same weekend or do I need to book in advance?

Book by Wednesday for weekend service. Last-minute bookings may not be available as weekend slots fill up quickly.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Just let me know as soon as possible. No cancellation fees — I understand things come up. We'll find another time that works.

What's included in event setup service?

Table & chair setup, decoration hanging, basic cleanup after your event. Perfect for birthdays, BBQs, or family gatherings. 2-hour minimum ensures enough time for proper setup and breakdown.

What areas do you serve?

Seminary, Oakland, Emeryville, Fruitvale, Coliseum, San Leandro, and nearby East Bay areas. Weekend service only (Sat & Sun).
